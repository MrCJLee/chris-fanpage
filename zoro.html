Zoro the 1st mate of the crew. He doesn't have any devil fruit, but he uses 3 swords to fight with. He likes drinking and working out. He will do any for Luffy and is very loyal to Luffy. He is very strong maybe even the 2nd strongest in the crew behind his captian Luffy. He lost an eye over the years, yet it hasn't stop from beating anyone that stops Luffy from becoming the pirate king. His goal is to become the world greatest sword man.
