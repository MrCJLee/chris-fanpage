Chopper

Chopper is a deer that ate the Hito Hito no Mi which means Human Human fruit. Chopper has the power of human intelligence, allowing him to understand and communicate in human language. The fruit has also given Chopper the ability to perceive concepts that a reindeer would not comprehend, such as medicine. It also allows him to transform into human/reindeer hybrid forms. Chopper is the doctor of the crew. Chopper goal is to become the best dcotor ever and cure cancer.
